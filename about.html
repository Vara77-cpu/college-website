About Us

GOKUL Group of Institutions were established by Mother Theresa's Educational Society in 2001 under the Chairmanship of Sri Kanjerla Lakshmi Narayana Yadav Former Minister, Govt of A.P., Chief patron of Hindu Public School, Junior, Degree & PG Colleges at Hyderabad,and chairman of Hanuman devastanam committee which has constructed a chain of temples in Sanath nagar, Hyderabad. NRI Engineers & Doctors from USA, UK, Singapore & Armenia are co-promoters. GOKUL is functioning from the academic year 2001-02 with a 4 year UG Courses, PG Courses, Pharmacy and Management courses. The Campus GOKUL has a large campus of 120 acres with natural green scenario, situated along R&B road and away from urban noise and pollution. GOKUL Institute of Technology and Sciences (GITAS) has been approved by the AICTE, New Delhi for 720 seats in B.Tech with 5 branches and 216 seats in M-Tech course with 10 specializations, 60 seats in MCA, and 60 seats in MBA courses. Further the 2nd shift Polytechnic college was also sanctioned for 480 intake. GITAS is Approved by AICTE New-Delhi, recognized by Govt. of A.P. and affiliated to JNTU, Kakinada. It is also accredited by National Board of Accreditation (NBA) by AICTE New-Delhi. GOKUL Group of Institutions has been approved by the AICTE, New Delhi, recognised by Govt. of A.P. and affiliated to JNTU, Kakinada. With intake of 360 seats in B.Tech, 60 seats in MBA, 60seats in B.Pharmacy,72seats in M.Tech, 72 seats in M.Pharmacy and 360 seats in Polytechnic (Diploma) GOKUL P.G. COLLEGE has been approved by the AICTE, New Delhi for 60 seats in MCA and 60 seats in MBA and recognised by Govt. of A.P. and affiliated to JNTU, Kakinada. GOKUL COLLEGE OF PHARMACY has been approved by the AICTE, New Delhi for 60 seats in B. Pharmacy 72seats in M.Pharmacy, 60 seats in D.Pharmacy and 60seats in Diploma in DMLT( Diploma in Medical Lab Technician Course) and recognized by Govt. of A.P. and affiliated to JNTU, Kakinada, this college is also approved by the PCI (Pharmacy Council of India) New-Delhi. GOKUL Degree College was established at Hyderabad with B.Com and B.Sc courses which is approved by Govt. of A.P and affiliated to Osmania University Hyderabad.
